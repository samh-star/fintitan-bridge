Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains what information is collected through this FinTitan FICA Tip Credit referral page and how it is used.

Last updated: July 9, 2026

Who Operates This Page

This page is operated by Sam Harding through Search and Marketing Solutions.

I am a referring agent for FinTitan and may receive a referral fee if your business becomes a FinTitan client.

If you have questions about this policy or your information, you can contact me at sams@agentmail.to or samh@getsams.com.

What Information Is Collected

If you choose to submit the form on this page, the information collected may include:

  • Your name
  • Your restaurant name
  • Your email address

Where The Information Is Stored

Form submissions are saved to a Google Sheet used to keep track of requests submitted through this page.

Why The Information Is Collected

This information is collected so I can keep track of your request and help connect it with FinTitan's review process for the FICA Tip Credit.

How Your Information May Be Used

  • To record your inquiry
  • To help connect your request with FinTitan
  • To communicate with you about your inquiry if needed
  • To help FinTitan understand that your request came through this referral page
  • To support the referral process if your business becomes a FinTitan client

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not use your information for unrelated marketing.
  • We do not share your information with unrelated third parties.

How Your Information Is Protected

Reasonable steps are used to protect the information you submit. Access is limited to those who need it to help with your inquiry or the referral process.

Your Choices

You are not required to submit the form. If you do not want to provide your name, restaurant name, or email address, you should not submit the form.

If you have submitted information and would like it updated or deleted, contact me at sams@agentmail.to or samh@getsams.com.

Important Notes

  • This page does not provide tax advice.
  • Sam Harding does not determine whether your restaurant qualifies.
  • FinTitan handles the detailed review process.
  • This page does not replace your accountant.
