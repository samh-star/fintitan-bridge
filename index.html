What Is the FICA Tip Credit?

The FICA Tip Credit is a federal employer tax credit that may apply to certain restaurants and hospitality businesses that pay the employer share of Social Security and Medicare taxes on qualifying employee tips.

This is an employer credit for the business. It is not a personal credit or refund for servers or tipped employees.

Could This Be Relevant to Your Restaurant?

This may be worth reviewing if your business fits one or more of these general descriptions.

  • Restaurants with tipped employees
  • Businesses that report tips through payroll
  • Employers that pay FICA taxes on qualifying tips
  • Restaurant groups with one or more locations
