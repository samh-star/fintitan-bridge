What This Is

The FICA Tip Credit (IRS Section 45B) is a federal tax credit available to certain employers in the food and beverage industry. If your business pays the employer share of Social Security and Medicare taxes on employee-reported tips, you may be eligible.

This is a business tax credit — not a personal tax credit for individual employees. It concerns taxes your business has already paid, and it may apply to the current year plus prior tax years.

FinTitan's team specializes in this credit and can review your situation at no upfront cost to see if you qualify.

  • Bars, nightclubs, and lounges
  • Catering companies and event venues
  • Hotels and resorts with food & beverage operations
  • Multi-location restaurant groups
  • Any employer that pays FICA taxes on reported tips
