For Restaurant Owners

An Employer FICA Tip Credit May Be Worth Reviewing

If your restaurant has tipped employees, there may be a federal employer tax credit opportunity connected to certain reported tips.

See How It Works

What Is This?

This is a possible employer FICA Tip Credit opportunity. It relates to qualifying employee tips and the employer share of Social Security and Medicare taxes that certain restaurants and hospitality businesses pay on those tips.

This is an employer credit for the business. It is not a personal credit or refund for servers or tipped employees.

Could This Be Relevant to Your Restaurant?

  • Restaurants with tipped employees
  • Businesses that report tips through payroll
  • Employers that pay FICA taxes on qualifying tips
  • Restaurant groups with one or more locations
