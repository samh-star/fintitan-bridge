What Is the FICA Tip Credit?

The FICA Tip Credit is a federal tax credit that may apply to certain restaurants and food or beverage businesses with tipped employees.

In simple terms, it is connected to employer Social Security and Medicare taxes paid on certain employee tips.

It is generally claimed using IRS Form 8846. It is not a new gimmick, grant program, or special loophole. It is part of the tax code, but whether it applies depends on the restaurant's actual situation.

The point of this page is not to give tax advice or tell you that your restaurant qualifies. The point is simply this: if your restaurant has tipped employees, it may be worth a closer review.
