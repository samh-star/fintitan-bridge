What Is the FICA Tip Credit?

FICA is simply the Social Security and Medicare tax employers pay.

Some restaurants may be able to claim a federal tax credit based on part of those employer taxes paid on certain employee tips.

This is a federal tax credit under IRS rules. It is not something FinTitan created.

This is a possible credit for the restaurant or employer. It is not a personal credit or refund for servers or other tipped employees.

Could This Be Relevant to Your Restaurant?

This may be worth a look if your restaurant:

  • Has tipped employees
  • Reports tips as part of its normal payroll process
  • Pays employer FICA taxes on those tips
  • Has one location or several locations
