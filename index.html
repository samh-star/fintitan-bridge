What the FICA Tip Credit Is

The FICA Tip Credit is a federal tax credit tied to IRS Section 45B.

In plain English, it relates to employer Social Security and Medicare taxes paid on certain reported employee tips.

That matters because tips are paid to your employees, not kept by the restaurant, but the restaurant may still pay employer payroll taxes connected to those reported tips.

This is a tax credit, not just a deduction. Whether it applies depends on your restaurant's actual situation.
